History

Built between 1852 and 1854 by Joseph Paxton and George Stokes for the Rothschild family in neo-gothic style.

Engineering Structure & Techniques

  • Thick stone foundations and Gothic pointed arches for load bearing.
  • Oak beam roofing system with metal reinforcements.
  • Modern restorations include moisture control, concealed wiring, and central heating additions.
  • Stained glass supported by lead rods, preserving wood and stone harmony.

Construction Cost & Inflation

The original construction cost in the 1850s was £150,000, which equals approximately £19.2 million GBP in 2025.
